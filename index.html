Screen Time

How much is too much?

Introduction

Technology is beautiful. The internet has defined a new age of humanity: the information age. Today we are connected in ways unlike those offered to any other generation. We can collaborate, learn, and play together without regard to where we physically are in the real world. Information travels at the speed of light. We have created an entirely new universe: the digital world, a world that has given us new ways to innovate and push forward. However, all of this is not without consequences. At the same time that technology helps us, it also distracts us. When constantly bombarded with an ebbing and flowing vortex of information that is the internet, it becomes increasingly difficult to focus our attention on one particular thing and get anything meaningful done. These distractions are why the average attention span of Gen Z is just eight seconds (McSpadden, 2015). Technology is a double-edged sword. The question is, how much time should children be spending in this digital world?
